## Getting Started

1. **Establish Your First Depot**
   - Choose a location from available towns
   - Cost: $30,000

2. **Purchase Vehicles**
   - Start with a delivery van for local routes
   - Expand fleet as business grows

3. **Hire Drivers**
   - Drivers operate vehicles and make deliveries
   - Each driver costs $200/day in wages

4. **Deliver Packages**
   - Packages generate automatically
   - Advance days to process deliveries
   - Earn revenue and build reputation

## Strategy Tips

- **Cash Flow Management** - Keep enough cash for daily operations
- **On-Time Deliveries** - Earn bonus revenue and improve reputation
- **Loan Repayment** - Pay down loans early to reduce interest costs
- **Fleet Optimization** - Match vehicle capacity to route demands
- **Hub Networks** - Unlock hubs to efficiently serve distant areas
- **Expansion Timing** - Grow steadily to avoid overextending finances
